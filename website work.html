Learn more about the beautiful game

Rugby is a close-contact team sport that originated at Rugby School in England in the first half of the 19th century. Rugby involves running with the ball in hand. In its most common form,referred to as Rugby Union, the game is played between two teams of 15 players each, using an oval-shaped ball on a rectangular field called a pitch. The field has H-shaped goalposts at both ends. Rugby Union also has a variation called Rugby 7s, which is played with seven players on each team, known for its fast-paced and high-scoring nature and also the less popular Rugby 10s, which is played with ten players on each team,a middle ground between 15s and 7s. There also exists a 13-man variation of rugby referred to as Rugby League which is more fast-paced than its counterpart. Rugby is referred to as the Gentleman's Game because of its emphasis on fair play and sportsmanship. The game is known for its physicality, strategic depth, and strong traditions of respect and integrity.
